Cinderella



  • The original Cinderella movie came out in February of 1950.
  • Cinderella is the first Disney Princess To not be of Royal Descent.
  • Cinderella was one of the only people whose foot could fit in the shoe because her shoe size was 4 1/2.
  • In the Cinderella movie, she was one of the first princesses to have a dog as her companion.
  • Cinderella is the second princess to run away from her love interest, the first being Snow White.
  • Cinderella is the first Disney Princess to have her name be a song title.
