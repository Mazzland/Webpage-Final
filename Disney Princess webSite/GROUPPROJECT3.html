Disney Facts

  • Pirates of the Caribbean and Tangled are two of the most expensive films that Disney has made so far.


  • Wall-E was named after Walt Disney , whose full name was Walter Elias Disney .


  • The Beast, from Beauty and the Beast, has elements of buffalo, gorilla, bear, lion, and wolf.


  • Cinderella was the first princess that was not from royal decent.


  • It is said that the song that Boo sings in the bathroom scene of Monsters Inc. is supposed to be "Tale as Old as Time" from the Beauty and the Beast.


  • Belle is the only disney princess that does not sing her movie's theme song.


  • Rapunzel is the only disney princess to have super natural powers.


  • Rapunzel's Hair is 70 ft. long.
